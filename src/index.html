Gone are the days of adapting your code to fit incompatible platforms. With our simple API, growing list of supported languages, and in-browser compatibility, Tupelo is the the first easy-to-use DLT protocol built to scale real-world applications.

Why Tupelo?
Simple. We’re different.

We’ve completely redefined the ledger with ChainTrees, an innovative data structure that gives every object its own standalone identity.

Traditional DLT platforms aren’t built for what you need. We are.

Us
  • Innovative

    Separates trust and storage and only keeps the latest, valid state without requiring total ordering.

  • Easy

    Modeled flexibly for ownership with a simple API, familiar language libraries, and cutting-edge SDKs.

  • Lightweight

    Every actor and object has its own independent ChainTree.

  • Secure

    No smart contracts needed.

Them
  • Outdated

    Unnecessarily stores the entire transaction history redundantly in the network and demands total ordering.

  • Complex

    Systems modeled rigidly for currency bogged down with unfamiliar blockchain development protocols.

  • Heavy

    Every actor and object exists together on one single chain.

  • Vulnerable

    Dependent on complex and sensitive smart contracts.
